More than being a woman in web development, or founder, or a woman in tech. I am someone who’s passionate.

Graduated from Broward College with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology with a concentration in Web Development. While finishing my degree, I also received certifications like CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management), CIW Advanced HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript and eCommerce Specialist. I was also the president of our Tech Club where every semester we worked in hands-on projects using frameworks/tools like ReactJS, React Native, Unity for Game development, Blender, Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and so much more. In 2019, me and my team were the BC Hackathon Winners for creating a Mixed Reality Art Gallery application using the Magic Leap One lenses.

By the time I graduated from Broward College, I had already been offered a job as an IT Project Manager. It was an amazing experience that unfortunately came to an end when Covid affected everything in our lives. I was working from home and my daughter who was 4 years at the time, was few months away from starting Kindergarten. As many parents, I had to make a hard decision to put my career on pause and help my daughter navigate a new school under new circumstances. That was when I decided to start my own Web Development Agency: Clever Fox
